Jane L. Ferguson

About Me

Jane L. Ferguson, LL.B, LL.M, is a Toronto lawyer practicing primarily for residential landlords before the Landlord and Tenant Board, Small Claims court and on appeals to Divisional Court. She is the author of a legal text on residential tenancies law and is a regular speaker at seminars on the topic. She acted as duty counsel at the Landlord Self Help Centre for approximately two decades and is now a member of its Board of Directors. She has taught Administrative Law and other courses at Humber College in the Paralegal and Law Clerk programs. She has been practicing since 1986 and obtained her Masters in Law from Osgoode Hall in Alternative Dispute Resolution in 2013.
Jane is an avid traveler and enjoys staying fit by running, cycling and yoga and has completed 18 marathons in various countries. She also enjoys pottery, gardening, cooking and wine.

About My Office
